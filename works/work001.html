Take Me Home Charity Event
Take Me Home 奔向幸福網路活動

Take Me Home was a charity bazaar event to raise money to help homeless animals.We made campaign video with two famous Taiwanese celebrities,cooperated with NGO Doghome.org 流浪動物花園 and many illustrators they came for donating art for this event.

To attract people we came up an idea that everyone came to this event using pen to write down messages for homeless pet.Then they can randomly get their own invented pet and see them walking on the website.(that you can see on the picture above).And We hypostatization our website to build a billboard wall and sticker machine to put on the works by our illustrators that people can interact with .And down below are some other event promotions we did.
